Resources For YouTubers

A list of free resources for online video creators

Music

Incompetech

Incompetech is a free music resource operated by music composer Kevin Macleod. Incompetech offers a large variety of royalty-free music that is licensed under the Creative Commons By Attribution 3.0 License, meaning that the only requirement for using said music is to give attribution.

Partners In Rhyme Free Music Library

Free music library provided by Partners In Rhyme for use in YouTube videos. Partners In Rhyme also has a music library available for purchase.

Zapsplat

Library of free music, most tracks require attribution for use.

YouTube Audio Library

Library of free music for use in YouTube videos provided by YouTube. Some music may require attribution, while others don't.

Free Music Archive

Library of freely licensed music licensed under a variety of Creative Commons licenses. Some licenses may prohibit commercial use, so check the license of a song before using it.

Sound Effects

Freesound

A collaborative database of Creative Commons Licensed sounds.

Zapsplat

Library of free sound effects, most sounds require attribution for use.

YouTube Audio Library

Library of freely licensed sound effects provided by YouTube.

Images/Video

Flickr Commons

Database of images from public photography archives provided by Flickr.

LibreShot

Catalog of Public Domain Images.

OpenPhoto

Catalog of images under various Creative Commons licenses.

Openclipart

Database of clipart images that are free for use.

Pixabay

Library of free images and videos.

Video Editing Software

Avid Media Composer First

Free version of Avid Media.

Avidemux

Open source video editing software

Blender

Free open source 3D modeling software, but is capable of video editing

Cinelerra

Free open source video editing software. Gnu/Linux only.

Davinci Resolve

Free editing software by Black Magic Design. Offers "Studio" version with more features.
